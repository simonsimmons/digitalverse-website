This is an interim privacy notice. A comprehensive privacy policy is being prepared with our legal team and will replace this notice once finalised. We've published this version so visitors have clarity about how their data is handled today. If you have any privacy questions in the meantime, email hello@digitalverse.com.au.

Who we are

Digitalverse is an Australian business based in Western Sydney that builds AI front-office systems for trade businesses. This notice covers personal information collected through digitalverse.com.au and our connected booking and live demo subdomains.

What we collect

We collect personal information when you:

  • Submit the booking form at enquiries.digitalverse.com.au — name, email, phone number, and any details you share about your business or enquiry.
  • Use the live demo at live-demo.digitalverse.com.au — your conversation inputs, which may include personal details if you choose to enter them.
  • Email us directly — your email address and anything contained in your message.

We do not currently use third-party analytics, advertising pixels, or tracking cookies on this marketing site. If that changes, this notice will be updated and a consent mechanism added.

Why we collect it

  • To respond to your booking, demo request, or enquiry
  • To configure and demonstrate Agentforge for your business
  • To keep in touch about your account, service, or relevant product updates
  • To meet our legal and operational obligations

We do not sell personal information.

Where it's stored

  • Booking form submissions — stored in our customer management systems via Vercel infrastructure and downstream tooling under our direct control.
  • Live demo conversation data — processed via the demo platform; not retained beyond the session unless you book or contact us.
  • Email — held in our standard business email systems.

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from misuse, loss, and unauthorised access, modification, or disclosure.

Your rights

You can:

  • Ask what information we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct or delete it
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Make a privacy complaint

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@digitalverse.com.au. We aim to respond within 14 days.

If you're not satisfied with our response, you can contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner at oaic.gov.au.

Updates

This notice is interim and will be replaced by a comprehensive privacy policy prepared with our legal team. The "Last updated" date at the top will change when material updates are made.
