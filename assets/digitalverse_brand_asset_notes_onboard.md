# Digitalverse Brand Asset Notes

## Updated tagline

**ONBOARD. SCALE UP. TRANSFORM.**

This replaces the previous **REBOOT. SCALE UP. TRANSFORM.** wording.

## Why this version works

**Onboard** is more practical, warm and customer-friendly. It aligns with Digitalverse’s first customer journey: understanding the business, capturing workflows, setting up systems, deploying agents and helping the team adopt AI safely.

**Scale Up** keeps the growth and leverage promise.

**Transform** keeps the aspirational edge while still feeling grounded when paired with Onboard and Scale Up.

## Production note

These files are high-quality raster assets based on the generated design direction. They are suitable for mockups, podcast artwork, social posts, decks and early website use. For final brand deployment, the logo should be recreated as clean vector artwork in SVG, PDF and EPS formats.
